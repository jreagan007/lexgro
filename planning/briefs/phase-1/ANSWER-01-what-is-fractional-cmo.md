## Content Outline

### Quick Answer (Featured Snippet Target - 50 words)
A fractional CMO is a part-time Chief Marketing Officer who provides strategic marketing leadership to your business on a flexible basis. Instead of hiring a full-time executive at $200K+, you get experienced CMO-level guidance for a fraction of the cost—typically 10-20 hours per month.
