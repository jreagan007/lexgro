## Terminology Reference

| Term | Definition | Use For |
|------|------------|---------|
| **Tagline** | Brand-level slogan (1 main one) | Logo lockups, headers, brand materials |
| **Value Proposition** | What you deliver + for whom | Hero sections, about pages |
| **Benefit Statements** | Outcome-focused phrases | Cards, lists, feature highlights |
| **Microcopy** | Short UI text, badges, labels | Buttons, badges, trust indicators |
| **Section Headlines** | H2s that frame content | Section headers throughout site |
| **Proof Points** | Credibility indicators | Social proof bars, testimonials |
| **CTAs** | Calls to action | Buttons, links, conversion points |

## 8. Voice & Tone Quick Reference

### Keith's Voice Markers
Use these phrases to maintain brand voice:

**Openers:**
- "Here's what most firms get wrong..."
- "The real question is..."
- "In my experience..."
- "What I tell every law firm I work with..."
- "Let me be direct..."
- "The truth is..."
- "Here's what actually moves the needle..."

**Closers:**
- "That's the LEXGRO difference."
- "It's that simple."
- "Now you know."
- "Ready to grow?"

### Tone by Section
| Section | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Hero | Confident, aspirational | "Predictable Growth. Without the Guesswork." |
| Problems | Empathetic, direct | "We know what's holding you back." |
| Solutions | Authoritative, helpful | "Here's how we fix it." |
| Features | Informative, exciting | "Everything you need to dominate." |
| Testimonials | Social, proof-driven | "Don't take our word for it." |
| Pricing | Clear, no-pressure | "Simple, transparent pricing." |
| CTA | Urgent, inviting | "Ready to grow?" |

## 9. Copy Don'ts

| Avoid | Use Instead |
|-------|-------------|
| "Best" / "Leading" / "#1" | Specific proof points |
| "Synergy" / "Leverage" / "Optimize" | Plain language |
| "Clients" (creates distance) | "Law firm partners" |
| "Leads" | "Potential clients" / "Cases" |
| "Sales" | "Intake" / "Client acquisition" |
| Vague claims | Specific numbers and results |
| Passive voice | Active, direct statements |
| Long sentences | Short, punchy copy |

---

## 10. Implementation Checklist

When writing new copy, verify:

- [ ] Does it sound like Keith would say it?
- [ ] Is it specific (numbers, timeframes, results)?
- [ ] Does it address a real pain point?
- [ ] Is there a clear outcome/benefit?
- [ ] Is it free of jargon and buzzwords?
- [ ] Does it build trust (proof points)?
- [ ] Is the CTA clear and compelling?
- [ ] Does it align with the brand voice guide?

---

## Quick Reference: The LEXGRO Copy Formula

**Problem → Solution → Proof → CTA**

1. **Acknowledge the pain:** "Inconsistent case generation is killing your growth."
2. **Present the solution:** "LEXGRO's Fractional CMO service builds predictable revenue systems."
3. **Prove it works:** "We've helped firms scale from $3M to $11M."
4. **Call to action:** "Get your free growth audit."
